Why Marbella
Marbella has recorded the strongest property price growth in Europe over the past decade — and shows no signs of slowing. International demand from Northern Europe, the Middle East, and Latin America continues to outpace supply. Benahavís, where CAVOK’s first project is located, sits at the apex of this market — a gated luxury corridor with restricted land supply, panoramic sea views, and consistent price appreciation. We chose Marbella not because it is popular, but because the fundamentals are exceptional.
