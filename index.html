01 — What is CAVOK

Dead capital doesn't move
on its own.
It needs infrastructure.

Real estate is the world's largest asset class — but it has always been difficult to divide, move or transact quickly. CAVOK Global is the infrastructure layer that changes that.


The platform structures European real estate as digital tokens on Flare Network. Each token represents a legal share in an SPV — a dedicated entity that owns the property. Everything is transparent and verifiable on-chain.


Not a fund. Not a wrapper. Not a promise. A new infrastructure layer for an asset class that has never had one.

02 — How it works

From property to
on-chain position.

CAVOK handles the complexity of structuring real estate on-chain. Each step is transparent, verifiable and enforced at the protocol level.

Step 01
Property selected
Due diligence completed. Asset structured into a dedicated SPV.
Step 02
Value attested
Property valuation confirmed on-chain via FDC Web2Json — no manual input.
Step 03
Tokens issued
SPV shares become tokens. 1 token = 1 verified legal fraction of the asset.
Step 04
KYC verified

Step 05
Position live
Ownership transparent and auditable in real time on Flare Network.

05 — Structure

Built for
compliance.

CAVOK tokens are profit-participating loan instruments — not transferable securities under MiFID II. The structure was designed with specialist legal counsel across Luxembourg SPV law, MiCA and MiFID frameworks.


Only KYC/AML-verified participants can hold tokens. Compliance is enforced at the protocol level, not just the interface.
