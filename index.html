01 — What We Do

Capital that works
before the first
brick is laid.

Most real estate investments lock your capital away while construction progresses. CAVOK deploys committed capital into DeFi vaults during the build phase — so your position may generate returns before the property is completed.

How it works
I
Vault yield — active from day one
Idle capital is deployed into DeFi vaults from the day funds are committed. Vault returns are variable and depend on market conditions. Distributed to token holders throughout the project lifecycle.
II
Rental income — conditional interest
Upon completion, rental income may flow on-chain as conditional interest on your loan position. Returns depend on occupancy and market conditions. Structured as profit-participating loans.
III
Exit — pro-rata distribution
Your share of proceeds at property sale. Partial exits supported — redeem any number of tokens. Exit values depend on market conditions and are not guaranteed.
