About

Built to make premium
real estate accessible.

CAVOK was founded on the belief that the best real estate investments shouldn't be reserved for the ultra-wealthy. We're using Flare Network to change that.

The European real estate market is one of the most compelling long-term investments available — but it has historically been locked behind enormous capital minimums, opaque structures, and zero liquidity for investors who commit.

CAVOK changes this by tokenising premium properties as profit-participating loans on Flare Network. Investors receive tokens representing their loan position, access multiple potential income streams, and can exit via our secondary market without waiting for the full project lifecycle.

"Idle capital is deployed into DeFi vaults from day one — before the first brick is laid."

Our first projects are in Marbella — one of Europe's most resilient luxury real estate markets. Monte Mayor Golf & Resort has seen consistent appreciation and strong rental demand, providing a robust foundation for our pilot launches.

The infrastructure is already built. 10 contracts verified on Flare Network. A working dApp. A tested investor flow. We are onboarding the first cohort of investors now.
